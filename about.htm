ABOUT ME:

The 3 most breath-taking things I've ever seen are: The Chagall window at the Art Institute of Chicago. The sun setting like a fire over the Grand Canyon. A full moon shining on winter mountains in the Bella Coola Valley.

My heros are Muhammad Ali, Atticus Finch and Princess Leia. I've been a full-time professional photographer for 8 years. It always amazes me how light can make anything beautiful. Dorothea Lange is my favourite photographer and my inspiration.

SOMETHING IMPORTANT:

I knew I was a photographer from the time I held my first camera. (It was a little, clunky Kodak 110.) I've been taking pictures for over 20 years now. It's what I love best. It's what I do best. That love goes into every one of the photos I take.

BEING ECO-FRIENDLY:

I'm committed to maintaining an environmentally aware, ethical business. All photos are distributed exclusively in digital format. I recycle and use recycled and sustainable materials. All of my professional lighting uses compact fluorescent bulbs. My business supplies are bought locally to Vancouver whenever possible

Shine & Rise Life Coaching

Certified Life Coach, serving Vancouver and British Columbia

Investing in yourself, always repays with interest.

Your future is shaped by making the decision to invest in yourself now. You improve the quality of your life, you find success, peace of mind and happiness by making your personal and professional growth a priority.

Options:

An excellent way to ensure your commitment to Life Coaching, is to purchase your complete Coaching package upfront. Or you can choose a monthly payment option, which available after your first 2 months.

What you get:

Three 30 to 45 minute phone sessions per month
A pre-coaching, personalized self-assessment form
A PDF exercise/homework assignment emailed to you after each session
A signed contract with options to extend

Life Coaching Sessions and Packages:
  • Free Consultation

    Your free 15 minute phone consultation is to give you some information about the process, and to determine the specific ways that Coaching can help you.

  • One-time Discovery Session

    This is a one-time, $75 session, 45 to 60 minutes. Together, we assess and clarify your needs and goals. Then we determine the best Coaching course of action for you.

  • Embark Package

    2 month commitment | $650 total
    3 sessions per month | 6 sessions in contract

  • Touchstone Package

    3 month commitment | $325 per month | $975 total
    3 sessions per month | 9 sessions in contract

  • Steadfast Package

    4 month commitment | $325 per month | $1300 total
    3 sessions per month | 12 sessions in contract

  • Encompass Package

    6 month commitment | $325 per month | $1950 total
    3 sessions per month | 18 sessions in contract

  • Extend and Customize

    For any commitment you choose, you can always extend your sessions by renewing your contract.
    After 6 months of Coaching, you have the option of customizing your sessions on an 'as needed' basis.

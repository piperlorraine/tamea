About Me:

I'm a CPC (Certified Professional Coaching) accredited Life Coach. My name is Tamea Burd. I'm also a Social Media Consultant and Professional Photographer. I've been operating my own successful businesses for over a decade. Each of them brings a different aspect of satisfaction and happiness. I became a certified Life Coach because I believe when we share our experiences and help each other, the world becomes a better place, one person at a time.

Shine & Rise Life Coaching

Certified Life Coach, Serving Vancouver and British Columbia

About me:

I'm a CPC (Certified Professional Coaching) accredited Life Coach. My name is Tamea Burd. I'm also a Social Media Consultant and Professional Photographer.

I've been operating my own successful businesses for over a decade. Each of them brings a different aspect of satisfaction and happiness.

My life opened up and got so much bigger and brighter when I realized we don't have to define our lives by just one endeavour. Our choices aren't a weight pulling us down, they're a foundation for our future.

I became a certified Life Coach because I believe when we share our experiences and help each other, the world becomes a better place, one person at a time.

About you:

You're here today because you're ready to live your best life. By knowing that, you're already on the right path.

With direction, encouragement and guidance; you can reach the place in your life where you want and deserve to be.

Prismatic Web Solutions is based in Vancouver, BC.

We offer our services across Canada.

We began as a website analysis service in 2007. Because most web-designers don't include SEO (Search Engine Optimization) services or use standards-compliant coding, we found that many websites suffer with very poor rankings in search results, or they look very different on different devices. To resolve website issues, hiring additional SEO and coding experts becomes extremely costly... We decided to offer an affordable alternative.

Over the past few years, social media has become a key factor in a strong online presence, but it can be overwhelming to start, create and maintain multiple pages on multiple websites. We also offer comprehensive Social Media Consulting and assistance.

As we're business owners ourselves, we know the importance of a well built, optimized website and a strong social media presence when it comes to search engine results and cross platform compatibility.

We also strongly believe that you shouldn't have to pay thousands of dollars for such an essential service, which is why we offer very reasonable, all-inclusive prices for all of our options.
